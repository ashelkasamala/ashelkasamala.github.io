About Me

I'm Ashel Kasamala, the creator behind AshTech—a digital space for smart, clean, and impactful solutions.

Currently pursuing a BSc in Electrical and Electronics Engineering at Information and Communication University (ICU) in Lusaka, Zambia, I enjoy blending engineering logic with creative design to solve everyday problems.

What I Do

  • Web Development: Clean and responsive websites using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and PHP
  • Database Systems: MySQL/XAMPP projects, DBMS designs for student or employee systems
  • Writing: Academic research, technical documentation, and assignments
  • Creative Design: Flyers, posters, banners—made visually appealing and brand-aligned
  • Embedded Systems: Arduino-based prototypes and simulation (Proteus)

I’m a hands-on learner and builder. Whether it’s coding a student portal, writing an academic report, or designing a compelling poster—I focus on simplicity, usefulness, and impact.

"My goal is to empower students and small businesses with the tools they need to succeed—digitally, creatively, and efficiently."
